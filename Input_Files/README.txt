Files structure :

V E
...
a b c
...

V = number of vertices
E = number of edges
a b c means that there is an edge from a to b that weights c

Files Hierarchy :

basic : handmade
simple : V in 5..20, E in 10..30
medium : V in 100..1000, E in 200..5000
complex : V in 1000..2000, E in 1000..10000
very_complex : V in 2000..5000, E in 10000..50000
huge : V in 5000..10000, E in 50000..150000
